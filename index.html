Ever since the beginning of published music, audio and visual creativity have walked hand in hand, inspiring each other to push the boundaries of a creative landscape.

Forage Press celebrates this ongoing creative union through visual based articles published both online and through a printed sine. The project invites creatives of all forms across the globe to produce original and exclusive artwork that is inspired by a musical subject matter of their choosing.

Creative Freedom

One of the main components of Forage Press is that it should be fair and free to all the participants. Each contributor is required to create a whole new series or single image based on any musical form they want, but thats where the rules end. Creatives are allowed to create anything they want without any creative input from Forage Press. Even the text that is submitted to accompany the visuals is not edited (unless creative asks for this) to ensure that none of the spirit or true voice from the creative themselves.

This creative freedom goes back to one of the original ideas behind Forage Press, to provide a creative spark and output for people who often spend most of their time working on client projects that have a lot of guidelines to them. It is important for our creative juices that we have opportunities to express ourselves sometimes, without limits of constraints.
